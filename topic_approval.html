Web Site Topic Approval


  • The purpose of my site is to inform and entice current, potential, and future patrons of the restaurant Buddha Bellies.
  • I want the web site to allure customers to come in to Buddha Bellies and order some of the fantastic food. The web site will also provide a menu, hours, and location.
  • The goal of my web site is to advertise, solicit, and showcase the restaurant Buddha Bellies to a broad range of future clientele.
  • My intended audience is male and female, ages 21-45, whom eat lunch out on a regular basis, work downtown, and enjoy eating new and exotic foods.
  • The opportunity my site is offering is for you to come into Buddha Bellies and order up some really tasty food.
  • My site will include a menu, images of entres, information about the restaurant, the cusine and the chef, hours of operation, location and map, a way to contact the restaurant, and a page for press releases, write-ups, etc.
  • List at least two related or similar sites found on the Web.
  1. Free House
  2. Grain and Gristle
  • The names of my pages and what information I will need:
  1. Home: I will need an image banner of the logo for the header and deliciously looking photos of the food they serve.

  2. Menu - I will need a list of menu items, a few pictures ingredients for those items, and a price list for those items.

  3. About - I will need information about the restaurant, the chef, and the cusine.

  4. Contact - I will need information about the location, hours, and how to contact the restaurant.

  5. Map - I will need a map with the location and proximity of the restaurant.
